REGULATORY GROWTH PATH

Building credibility the
right way, grade by grade

CIDB contractor grading is earned through demonstrated project track record and financial capacity — not claimed upfront. BESC is built to grow deliberately through this system.

WE ARE HERE
Grade 1
Entry registration. Projects up to R500,000. No track record required.
Grade 2–3
Projects up to R4,000,000. Requires our first completed, CIDB-registered projects.
Grade 4–6
Mid-tier projects, built on multi-year track record and financial capacity.
Grade 7–9
Large-scale and unlimited-value projects — the scale we are building toward.
This growth path applies equally to NHBRC residential registration and specialist professional sign-off (e.g. healthcare, large-scale commercial). BESC engages appropriately registered specialist professionals for any project requiring scope beyond our current registration.
